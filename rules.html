Library Rules & Regulations

Maintain Silence

  • Absolute silence shall be strictly observed inside the library at all times.
  • Mobile phones must be kept on silent mode; phone calls are not allowed in the library.[web:19][web:22]

Borrowing & Membership

  • Show your library ID card when borrowing or renewing materials.
  • Membership is non-transferable and borrowing tickets must be used only by the cardholder.[web:19][web:22]

No Food Or Drinks

  • Eating and drinking are strictly prohibited within the library premises.[web:19][web:22]

No Smoking

  • Smoking is not allowed anywhere inside the library.[web:19][web:22]

Personal Belongings

  • Bags, personal books, and other belongings must be left at the entrance or in designated areas.[web:19][web:22]

Respect Others

  • Do not disturb other library users. Group discussion is not permitted inside the main library hall.[web:19][web:22]

Library Timings

  • Follow all library timings and announcements displayed on the notice board or website.[web:19]
